About Me

My name is Gabe Gonzalez and I am a young UX/UI designer coming straight out of highschool. My goal is to become an even more experienced designer than what I have become at the UCLA UX/UI Bootcamp. Inspired by video games, sports, and music, I feel like my imagination can really run wild sometimes. Being able to put my imagination to work is what I love to do.I feel I have a general idea of what people like and don't like. For me, I enjoy creating designs that just make people say "Wow".

Area Of Focus

I'd definitely like to take what I've learned into the gaming, sports, or music industry. All of these have been in my life for as long as I can remember and I'd want to be working in a field that I can relate to. A field that I really just want to have pride in.
